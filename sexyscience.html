I am no scientist. When I did honors physics in high school, I made a very average bamboo trebuchet with my mom and, unbelievably, an even less successful mousetrap-powered car with my dad. And yet, my scientific misadventures have not deterred my love of science. And while I cannot truly explain Schrödinger's cat, as my friends will attest to, I will continue to pervade the field with my participation and commitment to the pursuit of knowledge.

These experiments are my illustrative response to the field of sexy science. They are not controlled. They are not, quote my flatmate, “very scientific”. They are here as a learning tool for those of us with brains perhaps more oriented towards the creative, or those who want a little more of a hands-on approach to science. (or maybe you just like to mouseover things to see what happens; I’m like that)

These trials may follow the studies upon which they are based, or they may completely flop. Such is the joy of testing things on the internet’s commonwealth without parameters.
